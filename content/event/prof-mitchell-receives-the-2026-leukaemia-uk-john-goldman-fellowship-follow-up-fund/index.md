We're delighted to have secured research funding from Leukaemia UK, in the form of a [John Goldman Fellowship Follow-on Fund](https://www.leukaemiauk.org.uk/research-and-advocacy/our-funded-research/using-computer-simulations-to-predict-when-lymphoma-will-return/). The award is focused on taking what we learned from the first project and bringing it closer to clinical use through the development of scores that predict the right treatment for the right patients.

Read more about the [award here](https://www.leukaemiauk.org.uk/research-and-advocacy/our-funded-research/using-computer-simulations-to-predict-when-lymphoma-will-return/).

We're so grateful to Leukaemia UK and all their supporters for believing in our work and supporting us to improve the lives of people with blood cancers.
